## Practical China Travel (Non-Chinese Speaker)

- **Payments**: WeChat Pay and Alipay setup for foreigners; cash as backup
- **Connectivity**: VPN must be set up BEFORE arrival (Google, WhatsApp, Instagram are blocked in mainland China)
- **Apps to install before arrival**: WeChat, Alipay, DiDi (Uber equivalent), Baidu Maps, offline translation
- **Getting around**: Bullet trains — book via Trip.com; DiDi for taxis; metro systems
- **Language tips**: Google Translate offline, screenshot menus, hotel address cards in Chinese
- **Food**: Picture menus, translation apps, pointing
- **Accommodation**: Hotels register foreigners with police automatically — carry your passport

## Hong Kong Specifics

- Separate from mainland — no VPN needed, Google/WhatsApp work freely, currency is HKD
- Octopus card for MTR metro and buses
- High-speed rail from West Kowloon station goes direct to mainland cities
