Intrepid Tour — China

Day 1
Hong Kong
Mon 19 Oct · Welcome meeting 6pm
▼
Temple Street Night Market Welcome meeting 6pm — MANDATORY
Welcome to Hong Kong! Meet your trip leader and fellow travellers at 6pm. If you arrive early, ride the Peak Tram for panoramic views, visit Tian Tan Buddha or stroll along Victoria Harbour. Tonight your group explores the lively Temple Street Night Market — try dim sum or roast goose for dinner.
⚠ Important: Harbour Plaza North Point is on HK Island — NOT Kowloon. Take MTR from Tsim Sha Tsui → Quarry Bay Exit C (~20 min). Leave hotel by 5:30pm. Bring insurance details and next of kin info.
Day 2
→ Chongqing
Tue 20 Oct · Bullet train ~9 hrs · up to 300 km/h
▼
Relaxed morning, then your first bullet train journey across China. Arrive Chongqing early evening — settle in or join your leader for an orientation walk.
Travel: ~9 hours. You can order takeaway food from train station to your seat (leader can help).
Day 3
Chongqing
Wed 21 Oct · Guided city tour + hot pot lunch
▼
Guided city walking tour Hot pot lunch in WWII air raid shelter Lunch included
Walk Longmenhao Old Street, stroll Nanbing Road skyscrapers, visit Hongya Cave, cross the Yangtze River by public cable car and ferry. Lunch is a traditional Chongqing hot pot served in a former WWII air raid shelter. Afternoon free — find the spot where the train goes through a building, or the street on the 22nd floor!
Day 4
→ Zhangjiajie
Thu 22 Oct · Bullet train ~4–5 hrs
▼
Orientation walk
Travel to Zhangjiajie in Hunan Province. Arrive mid-afternoon for a short orientation walk. Free evening — explore night markets, watch tai chi in the parks, try Hunan-style spicy food.
Travel: ~4–5 hours.
Day 5
Tianmen Mountain
Fri 23 Oct · Heaven's Gate · cliff walkways
▼
Tianmen Mountain visit
Visit iconic Tianmen Mountain with its cliff-hanging walkways, scenic cableway and Tianmen Cave (Heaven's Gate) — a massive natural archway. Spend a few hours exploring. Skip the 999 steps by taking an escalator to the top. Afternoon free in Zhangjiajie city.
Travel: ~1 hour each way.
Day 6
Zhangjiajie National Park
Sat 24 Oct · Avatar mountains · 4hr hike · Bailong Elevator
▼
National Park hike (~4 hrs) Bailong Elevator (glass cliff lift) Cable car
The towering sandstone pillars here inspired the floating mountains in Avatar. Hike through dramatic cliffs and forested valleys, then ride the Bailong Elevator — a glass lift built into the side of a cliff. UNESCO World Heritage site. Bring your camera.
Fitness: ~4 hours of hiking. Sturdy shoes essential.
Day 7
→ Changsha
Sun 25 Oct · Nvshu script workshop · night market
▼
Nvshu script writing with local master Night market visit
Bullet train to Changsha. Visit a cultural heritage centre to learn about Nvshu — the world's only writing system created exclusively by women, now a National Intangible Cultural Heritage. Try writing your own message. Evening at Changsha's night market — try sticky tofu, pig's feet or crayfish if you're brave!
Day 8
→ Shanghai
Mon 26 Oct · Bullet train ~5 hrs · Bund walking tour
▼
Leader-led night Bund walking tour
Morning bullet train to Shanghai (~5 hrs). Check in, free afternoon. Evening: leader takes you to the Bund — historic mansions on one side, modern skyline of Pudong across the Huangpu River on the other. Striking contrast of old and new Shanghai.
Travel: ~5 hours.
Day 9
Shanghai
Tue 27 Oct · French Concession walk · breakfast included
▼
French Concession walking tour Breakfast included
Morning walking tour of Shanghai's French Concession — tree-lined streets, European-style architecture, vibrant cafés. Free afternoon: consider Shanghai Museum (free), Yuyuan Garden & Old Bazaar, or the Shanghai Tower observation deck. Evening farewell dinner with the group.
Optional: Shanghai Museum (free), Yuyuan Garden (CNY40), Shanghai Propaganda Poster Art Centre (CNY25).
Day 10
Shanghai — Free Day
Wed 28 Oct · Second welcome meeting 4pm
▼
Second welcome meeting 4pm Shanghai Museum — Free Yuyuan Garden — CNY40 Propaganda Poster Centre — CNY25
Free day — wander Yu Garden, shop Nanjing Road or visit the Jade Buddha Temple. At 4pm, second welcome meeting to meet new travellers joining the next leg. Group and leader likely change today.
Note: New group joins today. Leader likely changes.
Day 11
Wuxi Day Trip
Thu 29 Oct · Huishan Ancient Town · Jichang Garden · breakfast included
▼
Huishan Ancient Town Jichang Garden Breakfast included
Half-hour bullet train to Wuxi. Walking tour through Huishan Ancient Town — cultural relics, historic sites, gorgeous gardens. Visit Jichang Garden, one of China's finest traditional gardens (national heritage site since 1998). Back to Shanghai in the afternoon — evening free, try a rooftop bar over the Bund.
Note: Jichang Garden may be closed for renovation — Huishan Temple and No.2 Spring Garden visited instead.
Day 12
→ Xi'an
Fri 30 Oct · Bullet train ~7 hrs · Muslim Quarter · breakfast included
▼
Muslim Quarter walking tour + snack sampling Breakfast included Great Mosque — CNY25 Bell & Drum Towers — CNY50 City Wall bike hire — CNY45
Bullet train to Xi'an (~7 hrs), once the imperial capital and start of the Silk Road. Afternoon walking tour: Bell and Drum Towers, Muslim Quarter, Great Mosque. Sample street snacks from local carts. For dinner: pao mo (lamb broth with flat bread), hand-pulled noodles, meat skewers.
Travel: ~7 hours.
Day 13
Terracotta Warriors
Sat 31 Oct · One of the wonders of the world
▼
Terracotta Warriors — leader led Tang Dynasty Show — CNY290 Big Wild Goose Pagoda — CNY80 Little Wild Goose Pagoda — CNY50
Journey outside Xi'an to the Terracotta Warriors — discovered after being buried for 2,000 years. Over 1,000 individually sculpted soldiers, each with a unique costume, height and facial expression. Commissioned by Emperor Qin Shi Huangdi in 264 BC. Free evening — Tang Paradise walking street near the Big Wild Goose Pagoda for local nightlife.
Travel: ~2 hours each way.
Day 14
→ Great Wall
Sun 1 Nov · Train to Beijing + transfer · sunset on the Wall
▼
Sunset on the Great Wall
Long travel day — grab snacks and a book. High-speed train to Beijing, then private vehicle to a family-run guesthouse inside the scenic area near the Great Wall. Tonight: highly recommended short hike to the Wall for an unforgettable sunset. One of the Seven Wonders of the World!
Travel: ~9 hours total (train + transfer). Guesthouse: basic but private facilities and Wi-Fi. In November, may be an alternate guesthouse with better heating — sunset hike may not be possible in this case.
Day 15
→ Beijing · Great Wall Hike
Mon 2 Nov · Jinshanling section · ~2hr hike · breakfast included
▼
Jinshanling Great Wall hike (~2 hrs) Breakfast included
Hike the Jinshanling section — one of the least touristy and most original stretches of the Wall. Watch it snake through the hills endlessly into the distance. Then head into Beijing for the final stop of the tour.
Travel: ~2 hours to Beijing. Fitness: Many steps and slopes — good grip shoes essential. Chairlift option available at own expense but closes in winter (Nov–Mar).
Day 16
Beijing · Tiananmen · Forbidden City
Tue 3 Nov · Farewell dinner — Beijing duck 🦆
▼
Tiananmen Square Forbidden City Temple of Heaven — CNY40 Hutong family visit — CNY150 Lama Temple — CNY25 Acrobat show — CNY290
Explore Tiananmen Square — the symbolic centre of Chinese power, framed by the Gate of Heavenly Peace, Mao's Mausoleum and the Great Hall of the People. Then enter the Forbidden City, built 500+ years ago, closed to commoners until recently. Explore halls and courtyards of the Ming and Qing dynasties. Free afternoon. Farewell dinner: Beijing duck!
Note: Forbidden City limited to 30,000 visitors/day — tickets may sell out. If unavailable, Temple of Heaven visited instead. Tiananmen occasionally closes without notice (especially March).
Day 17
Beijing — Tour Ends
Wed 4 Nov · Fly Beijing → Bangkok · CA959 14:00 PEK T3
▼
No activities planned — the China adventure ends here. Stay extra days in Beijing if you wish (arrange with your booking agent). Your Air China CA959 departs PEK Terminal 3 at 14:00 — leave hotel by 11:00am.
Airport: PEK (Capital), Terminal 3. Taxi ~45–60 min, ~CNY120. Or Airport Express from Dongzhimen (CNY25). Leave hotel by 11:00am.
