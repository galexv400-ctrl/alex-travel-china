## Key Packing Rules (Intrepid)

- **No aerosol sprays** on high-speed trains — roll-on/cream only
- **No pocket knives or scissors** in any luggage on trains
- **Power bank must be CCC/3-C certified** for China flights
- Luggage **under 15kg**
- Modest clothing for religious sites (cover shoulders + knees)
- Bring a day pack for daily excursions
